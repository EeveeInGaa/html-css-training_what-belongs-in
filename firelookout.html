What belongs in ...

... a Fire Lookout

Think of...

... a lonely place in the middle of the woods. Hard to reach but with a very nice view over the mountains. You are on your own. At the start of the fire season you go up the many stairs to sit the whole summer in your tower to prevent and report wildfires. From time to time you explore your surrounding, get supplies from the supply drop and look after hikers an if they behave correctly.
